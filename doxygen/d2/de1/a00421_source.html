29  * Template for Subject/Observer (or Publish/Subscribe). Offers flexibility
30  * in defining event types and having customized callbacks. Despite the
31  * shared_ptrs in the Register/Unregister, the Subject takes no ownership
32  * of the observer object and only stores a weak_ptr.
33  */
